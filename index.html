Our Story

Founded in 1985 by Italian immigrant Giovanni Rossi, Mamma Mia Pizzeria has been serving authentic Neapolitan-style pizza to the community for over three decades.

Our secret lies in our traditional recipes passed down through generations, our wood-fired brick oven imported from Italy, and our commitment to using only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients.

Every pizza is hand-tossed and cooked to perfection at 900°F for just 90 seconds, creating that signature charred crust and bubbly cheese that pizza lovers crave.
